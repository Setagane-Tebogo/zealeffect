About ZealEffect

Empowering South Africa's next generation of creatives through world-class, affordable media and music education.

Who We Are

A Creative Hub for
Media & Music

ZealEffect is a fast-growing, QCTO-accredited media and music academy established to promote and provide quality Media, IT, and Music education at low cost — particularly to previously disadvantaged communities addressing educational training backlogs.

We offer National Diplomas, National Certificates, FET Certificates, and Professional Masterclass courses in Sound Engineering, Studio Work, Film & Television Production, Radio Production, Video Technology, Music Performance, and a range of online distance courses.

If you are passionate about music, creative design, or the media industry — ZealEffect will help you build the foundation you need to make a success of your chosen career, in a creative environment full of like-minded individuals.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To offer all — irrespective of race, religion, or background — a quality education in the field of media, IT, and music technology. To be one of the leading quality education providers in Africa, offering NQF-approved and internationally accredited qualifications.

Our Mission

Got Questions?

FAQ

Who can apply to ZealEffect?

Anyone who is passionate about media, music, or creative technology — regardless of race, religion, or background. We welcome school leavers, career changers, and working professionals.

Are your qualifications nationally recognised?

Yes. All our full-time programmes are QCTO-accredited and registered with the Department of Higher Education. Short courses carry Steinberg and MICT SETA certification.

Do you offer funding or payment plans?

Yes — we offer various funding options and flexible payment arrangements. Contact us or visit our Funding page for details.

Do you offer online or distance learning?

Yes. We offer a range of online and distance courses alongside our campus-based programmes. Reach out to us to find out which options suit your schedule.

Will I get studio time during my course?

Absolutely. Hands-on studio experience is central to what we offer. Students get real lab and studio time as part of their training.
